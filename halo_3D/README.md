## About

This is an introductory course on 3D Computer Vision taught at the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore (NUS) by **Gim Hee Lee**.

The lecture covers:
- Existence of homography in planar scenes and pure rotation
- Direct Linear Transformation (DLT) algorithm for homography estimation
- Algebraic, geometric, and Sampson error
- Normalized DLT and data conditioning
- RANSAC for robust estimation
